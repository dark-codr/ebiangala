HRH (Dr) FAMOUS SAYEREGHA ESEDUWO

Director (Planning, Research & Statistics)

With renowned accomplishments within the Nigerian Public Sector, HRH (Dr) FAMOUS SAYEREGHA ESEDUWO has under his belt several Academic/Professional Laurels. He has been in the Nigerian Public Domain over the years as Local Government Chairman, Director-General Arts & Culture, Secretary, Bayelsa State House of Assembly Service Commission. Dr. Eseduwo is currently a Federal Director. He was also Honoured with the highest traditional title of his people as the Amananaowei of Angalabiri.

HRH (Dr) FAMOUS SAYEREGHA ESEDUWO, is known for his exemplary character and brilliant contributions to national and international Development.
