{% trans 'Brief History of Angalabiri' %}

Founded before the year
1815 AD by AKOU the son of Angalabiriowei and the grandson of Tarakiriowei.

Angalabiri has a traditional stool of Amananaowei (Paramount Ruler) which is duly recognized by the Bayelsa State Government and documented in the Gazette as a Second Class Stool. The Amananaowei is greeted as: "AKOU!, AKOU!!, AKOU!!!, Datimi Ondu" (Long live the Paramount Ruler).
